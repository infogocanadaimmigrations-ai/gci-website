About GCI

People first. Always.

Go Canada Immigrations was built on a simple idea: families deserve honest advice, not sales pressure. Twelve years later, that is still the whole company.

Our story

From one office in Model Gram to thousands of journeys.

We started in Ludhiana helping a handful of students reach Canada. We never grew into a faceless call-centre — we grew by telling people the truth, even when it cost us the deal.

Today GCI Global Pathways guides students to Canada, Australia, the UK and beyond — but the promise is unchanged: honest counsel, end to end, from people you can sit across from.
